    Session 3 2-4 Zarantyr 998 YK

    2 Zarantyr

    What a day!

    The group returns to Gallanda Hall as rain begins to fall across Sharn and it seems like Cligs has had quite the ordeal. She stays in the common room with Cassian and the two of them begin to get hammered. Caelin and S.A.M. bring the chest with the schemas up to their rooms and take a moment to investigate the documents. Unfortunately, Caelin does not appear to be able to read the documents nor determine what language they are written in. Unable to do anything further with these they join the others downstairs.

    A short while, and several drinks, later a House Sivis messenger arrives with a telegram for Cligs. The message is from Sergeant Vilroy and thanks them for saving her and says that she will stop by in the morning to deliver payment and will bring an expert to review the materials they recovered. With nothing left to do this day they continue drinking. In the course of the drinking Cassian seems to reveal that he may be hearing voices. The others are obviously concerned but S.A.M. steps in to redirect the conversation and try to stop Cassian from saying more - he also advises the young man to not openly say such things in the future.

    3 Zarantyr

    She's a squealer

    The morning arrives and, for most of the party, brings with it a hangover - and Sergeant Vilroy with a smartly dressed elderly human man. Cligs is obviously concerned about another potential changeling so challenges this Vilroy to share something only the real Vilroy would she - she does this by revealing that Cligs is a squealer in bed. Happy that this is the real Vilroy, she lets her continue speaking. She again thanks the group for saving her life and also renders their final payment for their task.

    She introduces them to Provost Bonal Geldem of the School of Pre-Galifar Studies at Morgrave University and asks them to show him the items they recovered. S.A.M. pulls out a selection of the documents and lays them out for the Provost who begins examining them. As he is reviewing them he becomes visibly more excited and invested in the document. Finally he reveals that these are ancient pre-Galifar schemas for House Cannith and that they are magically encoded so that they can only be read once you cast the correct spell codeword. He asks to take one with him and for the group to bring the rest to his office that evening and join him for dinner, they agree.

    After he departs the group has a few more questions for Vilroy but also establishes a secret phrase to use in case they ever need to verify if she is the real Vilroy or not: bugbear boogers. With that done, she leaves and the group finds ways to kill time.

    Goblin Delicacies

    The group takes this opportunity to visit the Bazaar District. Caelin, in particular, is looking for some new equipment. They visit Earoc's Blades, a weaponsmith, and The Golden Helm, an armorsmith, to purchase a new +1 crossbow and studded armor, respectively. While the group was waiting for Khuda, the dwarven armorsmith, to engrave the symbol of Aureon onto their armor, Cligs brings the group to her favorite goblin food stall and introduces them to Sharonda, a friend and the owner of the Green Skewer food stall. Cligs gets one of Sharonda's famous meat skewers for each of her new friends. They seem to enjoy their rat skewers.

    Another ambush?!

    By the time their dinner appointment arrives the skies have truly opened up and the rain is drenching as they make their way to Dalannan Tower to meet with Bonal in his office. On the bridge connecting Kelsa Spire to the University they spot what appears to be a body lying on the ground. Cassian quickly rushes over to see if the person needs help. He can immediately tell that the person is dead as a vicious wound is pouring blood out onto the skybridge and they can't detect any signs of life. Before he can make a more in depth study the group is attacked by a warforged soldier who bears the mark of Karnnath on her head and is wield a battleaxe. Cutter does not seem intent on listening to the group and dives into combat with gusto and spotting S.A.M. working with the "fleshies" calls him a traitor and focuses her ire on him. During the ensuing battle the group hears shouts from around them calling for the City Watch and, eventually, the shrill whistles that announce the coming of the Watch guards.

    The group is able to defeat Cutter and, as she falls in battle, they notice a small dragonfly-like construct emerge from her chest and begin to fly off. They try to catch it but are unsuccessful, so Caelin takes aim with their new crossbow and is able to hit and destroy the device. With both threats eliminated but the Watch closing in, they only have a few moments to examine the body. Cassian is able to determine that this is, unforunately, the man they were supposed to meet, Provost Geldem. Cligs notices a fancy journal in a leather pouch that he is clutching and quickly snatches it up.

    Possibly the worst detective ever

    In short order, three members of the City Watch appear pointing their weapons at the group and demanding they immediately lower their weapons and explain themselves. The person leading this group, a well-built dwarf by the same of Sergeant Dolom, doesn't seem to be the best at making connections but it earnest in wanting to understand what has happened ensuring that if the group was involved they are taken into custody. They are able to convince him that they didn't have anything to do with Geldem's death and were attacked by the warforged. He is able to tell them the thing they destroyed after Cutter fell was called a Final Messenger, which is a magic item that warforged can use to send a dying message to a specific person within range. The group also convinces Dolom to allow them to checkout his office with him to see if the item they had given him to look at was stolen.

    With S.A.M. distracting Dolom, the rest of the group is able to find out the following information:

    • Using a spell, Caelin is able to see where activity was within the past 30 mins within the office. Bright spots include what appears to be servants setting up the dinner in the office as well as right around his desk. They also see Bonal's fingerprints glowing from Cligs' bag.
    • They snatch up their schema that is sitting out on the desk as well as the notepad that Bonal had been writing notes in
    • Not remembered until later, but Caelin also realizes that there is one extra place setting at the table

    A cloaked stranger

    Having gotten what they can from Geldem's office and Dolom, the group head off to find a place to review their findings out of the rain and prying eyes. They head to the inn that Cligs works at, the Gray Dragon Inn, and sit down to discuss. Caelin shares what information they saw and they examine the notes Geldem had been making. It seems he had started to examine the schema, making notes about it's composition and properties, but wasn't able to decipher the codeword. Cligs also takes this time to share about taking the journal and they investigate this. It's a small (3x6") leather-bound journal that has what appears to be mithral threads woven throughout it's cover that converge into what resembles an ancient House Cannith symbol. Inside the pages appear to be blank and made of a special type of paper that isn't quite paper or leather. Cligs also detects what seems like Illusion magic within the journal.

    As they are discussing what to do next, with S.A.M. suggesting that maybe going to House Cannith is a wise course of action, a cloaked stranger enters the tavern and immediately comes up to them, as if they knew exactly who they were. When they approach the table they show a signet ring on their hand, which is that of House Cannith, and deliver the message: "If you would know the truth of Bonal Geldem's murder, go to the Broken Anvil tavern in Callestan at Dawn." Before anyone can respond or ask questions the stranger leaves. S.A.M. quickly gets us to follow the individual pausing in the doorway. He returns moments later and tells the group he didn't see where the figure went. Caelin is suspicious of S.A.M. behavior and realizes the bard isn't quite telling the full truth about what just happened. Unbeknownst to the group, S.A.M. had sent a Message to the figure.

    4 Zarantyr

    You know second cousin Kro?

    The group makes their way down to Callestan in Lower Dura, a deeply impoverished district of the city known for little City Watch presence and plenty of criminal activity. The Broken Anvil is an oddity in this environment because it is more of an upscale establishment, something you might find in a Middle Ward. As they make their way there in the early hours of the morning they come upon three Goblins blocking their path. It seems that these three Daask members had overheard information at the Broken Anvil about the group and suspects that they might be Boromar operatives. They decide to shake them down for information, and maybe some coin. However, Cligs recognizes their accents as being Darguun goblins from the same city she grew up. The lead goblin, Haraal, says he has a second cousin named Kro and asks Cligs if she knew them from back home. She lies and Haraal believes her. This "connection", along with a few silvers to ease the decision, convinces Haraal to let them pass and that they aren't Boromar, he gets permission from his Hobgoblin overseer, who was lurking in the shadows, Eskeen to let them go.

    The Aundarian Patron

    Making their way into the tavern they approach the halfling matron of the bar and request a table for 5. As she talks to them she glances down at a sheet of paper and back to them, it appears it may have their descriptions written on it. Recognizing the group, she leads them to a table in the back where human female with delicate features is sitting in clothing and adornment that surely standout in Callestan. As she greets them they notice that she is wearing a House Cannith signet ring as well. In the course of their discussion the group learns:

    • Her name is Elaydren Vown d'Cannith, from the Aundair Cannith West branch of the family
    • She was the extra person who was going to meet them with Bonal last evening and she learned from the Watch what had happened.
    • Bonal had been helping her locate a family heirloom from a lost Cannith foundry deep below the city
    • She offers them 1,000gp plus 100gp per diem and the favor of her House for recovering the heirloom

    When the group accepts, she asks for the journal. When her signet ring is placed on top of the seal on the journal all of the mithral glows brightly and suddenly the journal is filled with text and drawings. She flips to a specific page in the journal and consults it against a map she pulled out of her cloak. After a few moments she reveals the following information:

    • What they are seeking is an adamantine plate shaped in a seven-pointed star that is roughly hand-sized. It is a schema for an ancient Cannith design but on it's own it has no special power.
    • The lost foundry is 57 levels below Dorasharn Tower's present sewer system. The map will show them the way but first they will need to find the E-213 Valve Cluster to access the Dorasharn sewer system. Beyond that is a sealed door that should take them directly to the foundry.
    • There are other agents of the Lord of Blades about, having destroyed Cutter's final messenger may delay some of them but others will surely be looking for the schema still, and likely the group.
    • She also tells them that Bonal mentioned it would be important to have fire in the sewers but didn't know what for.

    Once the deals are set and the group is ready to wrap up, S.A.M. surprises them by giving Elaydren all of the schemas that he was storing that they recovered from Garra (all but the three being held by the other players). She seems grateful for this and hands them to a previously unseen aide. As the group leaves S.A.M. appears to linger behind the rest, ostensibly to give Errana, the halfling hostess, a tip. This raises Caelin's suspicions but they aren't able to determine that anything unusual happened and S.A.M. appears to be completely loyal to Cassian. Unbeknownst to the group, he had sent another secret Message, this one to Elaydren.

    Before they head down to the sewers and beyond, the group makes a second quick trip to the Bazaar to acquire a bag of holding and 3 Alchemist Fire potions.
